# Law 2 Conclusion: Never Put Too Much Trust in Friends

Forget the title. Remember the core idea:

**Closeness can blind you. Always look at the rope pulling a person's behavior.**

People are not only moved by friendship. They are moved by:

- ego
- money
- fear
- status
- comfort
- ambition
- loyalty
- insecurity

A friend can love you and still make a bad decision.

A stranger can help you because their incentives align with yours.

So don't judge only by:

> "How close is this person to me?"

Judge by:

> "What force is pulling this person to act this way?"

## Your Memory Image

🪢 Every person has an invisible rope.

Their actions follow the rope.

Find the rope, understand the action.

## The Final Sentence

> **"Don't trust the face; understand the force behind the face."**

That's Law 2.
