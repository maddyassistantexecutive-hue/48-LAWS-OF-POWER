## Law 7 Conclusion: Get Others to Do the Work for You (Leverage)

Forget the title.

The real lesson:

> **Don't only increase your own effort. Increase the force behind your effort.**

Your mistake would be thinking:

> "I need to become the strongest person."

Law 7 says:

> "Become the person who can move the most."

---

### Memory Image 🎮

A pistol:

* One person
* One direction
* Limited bullets

An M416:

* More output
* More capacity

But a commander:

* Controls many weapons
* Multiplies results

**Law 7 is becoming the commander.**

---

### The Question to Remember:

When something needs to be done, don't ask:

> "How can I do all of this?"

Ask:

> **"What system, tool, or people can multiply this?"**

---

### The Trap

Don't misunderstand:

It doesn't mean:

> "Use people."

It means:

> **Learn to create results beyond your personal limits.**

Good leaders:

* Build teams
* Teach others
* Create systems
* Share credit
* Coordinate effort

---

### Final Line:

> **"A person who only works has a limit. A person who creates leverage has no obvious limit."**

Law 1 → respect ego
Law 2 → find the rope
Law 3 → hide the map
Law 4 → control words
Law 5 → protect reputation
Law 6 → get seen
Law 7 → multiply force

That's Law 7.
