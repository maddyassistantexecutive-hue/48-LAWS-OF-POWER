## Law 10 Conclusion: Avoid the Unhappy and Unlucky

Forget the title.

The real lesson:

> **Don't just look at a person — look at the pattern that follows them.**

A person can have problems. That's normal.

The danger is the person who repeatedly creates the same disasters and never changes.

---

### The Rule

Don't ask:

> "Is this person nice?"

Ask:

> **"What happens when this person enters a situation?"**

Do things improve?

Or does chaos follow?

---

### Memory Image 🌧️

Everyone gets caught in rain.

But some people:

* build a roof
* learn
* move forward

Others:

* carry the storm everywhere
* blame the sky
* pull others into the rain

---

### The One Sentence:

> **"Don't judge people by their storms; judge whether they learn to stop creating them."**

Your first 10 laws now:

1. Respect people's ego
2. Find the invisible rope
3. Protect your map
4. Control your words
5. Guard your reputation
6. Become visible
7. Multiply effort
8. Become the attraction
9. Let actions speak
10. Watch the patterns people create

That's Law 10. Keep these 10 in your head; they already form a strong way of reading people.
